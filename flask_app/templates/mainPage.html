Empowering Tourism in Korce through Innovative Web Application.

Anxhela Fecanji, a talented graduate from the European University of Tirana, has embarked on a mission to showcase the hidden gems of Korce, Albania, through her innovative web application. Currently a student at the American Coding Dojo academy, she collaborated with BetaPlan in Albania to create a platform dedicated to promoting tourism in her beloved city.

Korce, a small city boasting rich historical and cultural heritage, captivates visitors with its vibrant traditions and welcoming atmosphere. Despite its size, Korce stands as one of Albania's top tourist destinations, drawing both locals and foreigners alike. The city's allure transcends seasons, yet it holds a unique charm during the winter months, making it a must-visit destination.

The web application curated by Anxhela Fecanji serves as a comprehensive guide for tourists, offering insights into local attractions, activities, accommodations, dining options, and more. From historical landmarks to cozy cafes, the platform provides a glimpse into the diverse offerings of Korce, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in its cultural tapestry.

Through her dedication and expertise in Information Technology, Anxhela Fecanji has crafted a digital gateway that not only promotes tourism but also celebrates the essence of Korce.
